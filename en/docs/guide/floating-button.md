## Overview

The floating button is a quick entry on supported platforms. It opens the sidebar and core features, and lets you customize click actions and hover drawer items.

![Floating Button](/ui-images/ui-悬浮按钮.png)

## How to Use

1. Open any supported AI chat page
2. The Chat Copilot floating button appears in the lower-right corner
3. Click it to open the sidebar and access features like Prompt Plaza and My Favorites

## Common Actions

- Open the sidebar: click the floating button
- Quick access: open [Prompt Plaza](/en/docs/guide/plugin-square.html) and [My Favorites](/en/docs/guide/plugin-favorite.html) from the sidebar
- Close or collapse: use the close or collapse button in the sidebar

## Related Settings

Manage the floating button in: `Plugin Panel → Settings → Floating Button`.

### Display Toggle

Turn on "Show Floating Button" to display the Chat Copilot logo on the right side of AI websites.

### Click Action

Configure what happens when you click the floating button:

- No action
- [Plugin Settings](/en/docs/guide/plugin-option.html)
- [Prompt Plaza](/en/docs/guide/plugin-square.html)
- [My Favorites](/en/docs/guide/plugin-favorite.html)
- Prompt Optimization

### Hover Drawer

When you hover over the floating button, a top drawer appears. You can choose which items to show:

- [Prompt Plaza](/en/docs/guide/plugin-square.html)
- [My Favorites](/en/docs/guide/plugin-favorite.html)
- [Plugin Settings](/en/docs/guide/plugin-option.html)
- Official Website
